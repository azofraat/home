Timeline

YEAR EVENT
Franchise is awarded to a group headed by Toronto businessmen John Biltove. November 4th 1993
Nationwide naming contest ends with Raptors. May 15th 1994
Expansion Draft. The Raptors pick their first roster, with B.J. Armstrong being the first player ever on the team. June 24th 1995
Raptors draft Damon Stoudamire 7th overall. June 28th 1995
Raptors play the first game, an exhibition game against The Philadelphia 76rs. Oct 14th 1995
Raptor play their first regular season game. November 3rd 1995
Damon Stoudamire Wins Rookie of the Year and The Toronto Raptors end their first season with a record of 21-61. Spring 1996
Construction of new Stadium begins: Air Canada Center. March 12th 1997
Maple Leaf Gardens Inc. Buys The Raptors and the Air Canada center. February 1998
Raptors acquire Vince Carter. June 1998
Air Canada Center opens. February 1999
Carter becomes first Raptor ever in an all-star game. February 2000
Raptors make their first ever playoff appearance. April 2000
The Toronto Raptors make it past the first round for the first time in history. Spring 2001
Toronto drafts number four overall pick Chris Boch. Spring 2003
Vince Carter gets traded to the New Jersey Nets. December 2004
The Toronto Raptors draft dissapointing Andrea Bargnani with the first pick. June 2006
The Raptors select DeMar DeRozan ninth overall. Summer 2009
Chris Bosh signs with Miami to join Lebron James and Dwayne Wade. April 2010
Attempting, but failing, to draft Steve Nash, the team aquires Kyle Lowry from the Huoston Rockets. Summer 2012
Masai Ujri becomes the president of the franchise. April 2013
In a marketing effort Drake signs with the Raptors to be the team's global ambassador. September 2013
A playoff drought of six seasons comes to an end. The Raptors fall in seven games to the Brooklyn Nets. Spring 2014
The NBA All-Star Game is played at the Air Canada Centre in Toronto. The first time the contest is held outside of the U.S. February 14th 2016:
Nick Nurse takes over as head coach for the team. Spring 2018
Masai makes a risky trade, giving away Demar Derozan and Jakob Poeltle for Danny Green and Kawhi Leonard. July 18th 2018
Another risky trade brings veteran Marc Gasol to the Raptors. February 8th 2019
With seconds remaining in Game 7 of the second round series against Philadelphia, Kawhi hits a historic buzzer-beater from the corner. May 12th 2019
The Raptors make it for the first time ever to the NBA finals. May 25th 2019
In six games the Raptors, for the first time in history win the NBA. Kawhi is voted finals MVP. June 14th 2019
